About me

Hello, I’m Daniel — a 31-year-old game art and animation student, and a bit of a late bloomer when it comes to creating art. I’m currently a sophomore at the University of Advancing Technology, where I’m developing my skills in 3D modeling, animation, and digital design. Before pursuing this path, I spent six years serving in both the Navy and the Army — experiences that taught me resilience, leadership, and the importance of continuous growth. Transitioning from military life to digital art has been a major shift, but one that’s helped me rediscover my creativity and sense of purpose. Each project I take on is another step toward mastering my craft and building the technical and artistic foundation needed to bring ideas to life in the game industry.
